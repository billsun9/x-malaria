Uploading multiple files:

When uploading multiple files, follow these instructions:

1. Click on Upload link in red navigation bar on the top of the page

2. Fill out the brief survey

3. Upload the file. Upload multiple files by holding "ctrl" and clicking on multiple files

4. Click submit, and see the result.



Note: as the models for detection and classification were trained on thin blood smear images that were stained with the wright-giemsa reagant, the model should take input images that were also stained by that reagant. Otherwise, the model will likely be unable to correctly detect the RBCs and/or be unable to correctly classify them.
